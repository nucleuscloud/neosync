Self-hosting Neosync is a great way to meet various compliance requirements and/or ensure that your data never leaves your infrastructure.

We highly recommend that you deploy Neosync on Kubernetes but we do make Neosync available to deploy using Docker Compose for non-Kubernetes related deployments. Click on one of the options below to get started.
